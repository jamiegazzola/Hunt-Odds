How it works
Three steps to finding the right draw for you.
01
Filter by species & region
Select one or multiple species — Moose, Elk, Deer, Sheep and more. Narrow by management unit to focus on areas you actually want to hunt.
02
Set your minimum odds
Use the odds slider to filter out the toughest draws. Focus only on entries where you have a realistic chance of drawing a tag.
03
Sort & compare
Sort by best odds or by season date. Each card shows you the area, MU, drive time from White Rock, and full season details.

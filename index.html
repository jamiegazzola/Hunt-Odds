How it works
Four steps to hunting smarter.
01
Choose your province
Select British Columbia or Alberta. Each province has its own draw system, data, and odds — we cover both.
02
Set up your Alberta profile
For Alberta, enter your residency status and priority points by category once. The app uses your profile to show your personal odds across every draw.
03
Filter by species, region & odds
Narrow results by species, wildlife management unit, and minimum draw odds. Find draws where you have a realistic shot.
04
Explore personalized results
Each draw card shows historical success rates, draw odds by year, and for Alberta — your personal odds at your priority level.
